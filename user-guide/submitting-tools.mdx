Contribute to the AiVault directory by submitting AI tools you've discovered or created. All submissions are reviewed by our team before being published.

## Requirements

<Warning>
You must be signed in with a verified account to submit tools. Create an account or sign in before proceeding.
</Warning>

## Submission Process

The submission form is divided into 3 steps to make it easy to provide comprehensive information.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Basic Information">
    Provide essential details about the tool:
    
    ### Required Fields
    
    - **Tool Name**: The official name of the AI tool (e.g., "ChatGPT")
    - **Website URL**: The primary website URL for the tool
    - **Short Description**: A brief one-liner explaining what the tool does
    - **Category**: Select from Writing, Image, Video, Coding, Productivity, Audio, or Design
    - **Pricing**: Choose Free, Freemium, or Paid
    
    ### Optional Fields
    
    - **Logo**: Upload an image (max 2MB) or provide a URL
      - You can use URL services like `https://logos.hunter.io/yourdomain.com`
      - Switch between URL and file upload using the toggle buttons
    - **Detailed Description**: A longer explanation of capabilities and unique features
    - **Pricing Details**: Specific pricing information (e.g., "Free tier available. Pro from $20/mo")
    
    <Tip>
    A good logo makes your submission more visually appealing and easier to recognize in the directory.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Features & Details">
    Add rich details to make your listing stand out:
    
    ### Required
    
    - **Tags**: Add at least 1 tag (type and press Enter to add each tag)
      - Tags help users discover your tool through search
      - Choose descriptive keywords related to functionality
    
    ### Optional
    
    - **Platforms**: Select all that apply
      - Web, iOS, Android, Desktop, API, Chrome Extension
    - **Key Features**: List standout capabilities
      - Example: "Real-time collaboration", "AI-powered suggestions"
    - **Use Cases**: Describe practical applications
      - Example: "Content writing for blogs", "Social media captions"
    - **Pros**: Highlight strengths and advantages
    - **Cons**: Note limitations or areas for improvement
    
    <Note>
    Being honest about pros and cons builds trust and helps users make informed decisions.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Links & Submit">
    Add social and community links (all optional):
    
    - **Twitter URL**: Link to official Twitter/X account
    - **GitHub URL**: Repository or organization page
    - **Discord URL**: Community server invite
    
    Review your submission and click **Submit Tool** when ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Submission

### Email Confirmation

You'll receive an email confirmation that your submission was received successfully.

### Review Process

Our team reviews all submissions to ensure:

- Tool information is accurate and complete
- The tool is relevant to the AI/ML space
- Content meets community guidelines
- No duplicate listings exist

<Note>
The review process typically takes 1-3 business days. You'll receive an email when your tool is approved or if we need additional information.
</Note>

### Approval

Once approved:

- Your tool appears in the public directory
- Users can discover, upvote, and bookmark it
- It becomes searchable and filterable
- You can view it from your dashboard

### Tracking Your Submissions

View all your submitted tools from your user dashboard, including:

- Pending submissions awaiting review
- Approved tools in the directory
- Submission status and timestamps

## Tips for Great Submissions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Write Clear Descriptions">
    Focus on what the tool does and who it's for. Avoid marketing jargon and be specific about capabilities.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Choose Relevant Tags">
    Think about search terms users might use. Include technology types (GPT, Stable Diffusion) and use cases (blogging, design).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Add Rich Details">
    The more information you provide (features, use cases, platforms), the more useful the listing is to potential users.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Include Social Links">
    Twitter, GitHub, and Discord links help users connect with the tool's community and stay updated.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use Quality Logos">
    Clear, high-resolution logos make your tool stand out in the directory grid. PNG or SVG formats work best.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
You can submit tools you've created or discovered. If you're submitting someone else's tool, make sure you have accurate information.
</Tip>
